## Abstract
There have been many initiatives for data rescue in the US as the federal government cuts staff and funding for many services. While much focus has been on health, climate, and social justice datasets, there's little said about potential or actual loss of indigenous data. This presentation will focus on the Repository Crisis Scorecards, a tool for assessing risks to a repository that could lead to data loss, and how to judge risks to where data is stored, indigenous or not.
