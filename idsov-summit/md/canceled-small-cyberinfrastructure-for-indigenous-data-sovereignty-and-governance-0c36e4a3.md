## Abstract
Small cyberinfrastructure is defined as processes for individual people, labs, communities. What are solutions for managing data for your self, your lab, your family, your neighborhood? What hardware or computers are better suited for storing data? What software has better privacy and security and respects user sovereignty? What alternatives are people using to cloud service providers, e.g. Google Docs, Microsoft Teams? This roundtable seeks to bring people together concerned about their own practices in managing their data so that they can share challenges, successes, and experience with others.
