# Tribal Technology Assessment Tools

## Presenter(s)
- Adae Briones

## Date/Time
Thursday, Apr 16, 2026 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM MST

## Location
Junior Ballroom J Heeka

## Abstract
This session focuses on the viability of a Peer Technology Assessment Tool for Tribal Communities. The workshop will be a two part session. The First part of the workshop will walk participants through the Peer Technology Assessment Tool as it is originally designed. The second part of the workshop will work with Tribal participants to get feedback on the viability and adaptions needed to make it relevant for Tribal communities.
